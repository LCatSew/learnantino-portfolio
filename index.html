About Me

My name is Lily Sewell, but I prefer to go by LC. I am 25 and originally from Detroit Michigan!
I moved to Austin TX in August of 2020 on a whim after quitting my job as a hairstylist. I became involved with a great non-profit called Festival Beach Food Forest.
Their mission is to provide community, green space, and free food with as little barrier as possible to underserved Austin residents.
This lead me to persuing a career in a furniture company that focuses on producing an adaptable, changeable product that does not need to be replaced.
I am one of the youngest department managers in my company- and the only one in my age range without a college degree.
The decision to learn how to code came naturally to me. I pride myself on my pattern recognition, quick learning, and attention to detail.
